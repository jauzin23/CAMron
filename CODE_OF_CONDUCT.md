# Code of Conduct

We want to build a welcoming and friendly community for everyone. As a contributor or maintainer of this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct to help keep the environment respectful and safe.

## Our Standards

Please treat everyone with respect and kindness. We expect all participants to be helpful, professional, and welcoming to others, regardless of their experience level, background, or identity.

Do not use offensive, abusive, or discriminatory language. We do not tolerate harassment, personal insults, or any form of disrespectful behavior.

## Responsibilities of Maintainers

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing our standards. They have the right and responsibility to edit, reject, or remove comments, issues, code, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by sending an email to jaam.joao.monteiro.2008@gmail.com.

We will review all reports and take the necessary actions to address the situation. We will keep the identity of the person reporting the issue private.
